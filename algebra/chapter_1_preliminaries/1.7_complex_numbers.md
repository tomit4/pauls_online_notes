**Various Powers of $i$**

$$ i^1 = i $$

$$ i^2 = -1 $$

$$ i^3 = i \cdot i^2 = -i $$

$$ i^4 = (i^2)^2 = (-1)^2 = 1 $$

$$ i^5 = i \cdot i^4 = i $$

$$ i^6 = i^2 \cdot i^4 = (-1)(1) = -1 $$

$$ i^7 = i \cdot i^6 = -i $$

$$ i^8 = (i^4)^2 = (1)^2 = 1 $$

---

**Rules regarding square roots of negative numbers**

$$ \sqrt{a}\sqrt{b} = \sqrt{ab} $$

$$ \sqrt{-a}\sqrt{b} = \sqrt{-ab} $$

$$ \sqrt{a}\sqrt{-b} = \sqrt{-ab} $$

$$ \sqrt{-a}\sqrt{-b} \neq \sqrt{(-a)(-b)} $$
